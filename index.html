Intro

I was born in 1995 in France. I have done my studies at Angers University. After a licence and Master, I started my PhD thesis on the Teichmüller space of \begin{equation} \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})/\Gamma \end{equation} under the supervision of Laurent Meersseman and Marcel Nicolau.
I am currently in ATER (Attaché temporaire d'enseignement et de recherche) at the Angers University,
